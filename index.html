Welcome

"Gargarro Botanic Garden Cafe is more than just a cafe. It's a hub for the community, a place where neighbours can catch up, a respite from a road trip, or a venue for your next function."

Welcome to Gargarro Botanic Garden Cafe, a licensed café set against the scenic and unhurried backdrop of Gargarro Botanic Garden in Girgarre, VIC. The unique double-story building offers expansive views of the surrounding botanic gardens, making it the perfect place to relax, be serenaded by local birds and soak in the natural beauty of the area.

Community

A creative community that thrives on new intiatives. Girgarre is a small town with a big spirit

Botanic Garden

Gargarro Botanic Garden is the jewel in the crown of the Campaspe region. The planned project is to create a bold, beautiful and best practice garden, celebrating indigenous history in a parkland that stimulates the senses.
