Welcome to Gargarro Botanic Garden Cafe, a licensed café set against the scenic and unhurried backdrop of Gargarro Botanic Garden in Girgarre, VIC. The unique double-story building offers expansive views of the surrounding botanic gardens, making it the perfect place to relax, be serenaded by local birds and soak in the natural beauty of the area.

Owners, Joel and Leon, bring their extensive experience in hospitality and coffee-making to the Gargarro Botanic Garden Cafe . They've been serving up delicious cups of coffee at local markets for years, and now – with the help of staff members from Girgarre and neighbouring towns -- are dedicated to providing an inviting atmosphere at the café, where customers can feel right at home.

Gargarro Botanic Garden Cafe takes pride in sourcing many of their ingredients from local farmers and suppliers. The menu features a range of classic cafe fare, from hearty breakfasts to tasty lunches, as well as locally made cakes and sweets. Patrons can also choose from a selection of alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages, coffee, tea, and an impressive array of hot chocolates to help start their day or unwind after a busy one.

Looking for a unique dining experience? Gargarro Botanic Garden Cafe is open late on Friday and Saturday nights, serving up pizzas, grazing boards, antipasto platters, and, of course, a selection of delicious beverages. Gargarro Botanic Garden Cafe is more than just a cafe. It's a hub for the community, a place where neighbours can catch up, a respite from a road trip, or a venue for your next function. Customers can come in and be treated to the best food, drink, and hospitality in Girgarre, a small town with a big spirit.
